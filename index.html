About Me

I'm Dr. Hosna Ghandeharioun, a data scientist, machine learning expert, and an esteemed researcher in biomedical engineering with 20 years of dedicated experience in academia and industry as a university professor and biomedical engineering consultant.

I am pushing the boundaries of healthcare technology to new heights using Artificial Intelligence.With a solid foundation in biomedical signal processing, pre-diagnostic system development, and more, I'm deeply passionate about employing blind source separation analysis, deep learning, and Generative AI across diverse medical domains.

Some of my notable achievements include Electroencephalogram (EEG) ocular artifact removal and home-based pre-diagnosis of obstructive sleep apnea (OSA), where I contributed significantly to novel data analysis methodologies and pre-diagnostic systems. In the realm of research and project development, I thrive from concept inception to seamless management, ensuring impactful outcomes.

My approach goes beyond analytical thinking; I'm a dedicated problem solver, an innovative force, and a collaborative spirit. I have founded and directed the Biomedical Engineering & Affective Computing Lab at the Department of Electrical and Biomedical Engineering in Khorasan Institute of Higher Education (KIHE) and established and supervised the IEEE Student Branch in International Imam Reza University (IRIU).
